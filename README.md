# Fuji Tyres

Premium tyres. Expert advice. Unbeatable prices.

Fuji Tyres is your trusted destination for quality tyres and rims in Lagos, Nigeria. We stock over 50 top global brands and provide expert consultation to help you find the perfect match for your vehicle and driving needs.

## What We Do

- **Tyre Sales** — Wide selection of premium tyres for cars, SUVs, trucks, and commercial vehicles. All sizes and budgets covered.
- **Tyre Rims** — Quality alloy and steel rims, from factory replacements to stylish custom designs.
- **Expert Consultation** — Free personalised advice to help you choose the right tyres and rims for your vehicle.
- **Tyre Inspection** — Comprehensive tyre health checks including tread depth, pressure, and sidewall condition.

## Brands We Stock

Michelin, Bridgestone, Continental, Pirelli, Goodyear, Dunlop, Hankook, Yokohama, Firestone, BFGoodrich, and many more.
